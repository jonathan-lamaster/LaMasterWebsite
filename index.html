Web Development

Trusting your website development can be a overwhelming task. Individuals and Small Business invest thousands of hours of work into their ideas, and want a developer who will share the same dedication to building the representation of that work on the web. I approach each project with that goal as the focus, and deliver clean mobile responsive websites that highlight your business.

HL7 Development

HL7 interface development is a highly specialized corner of the Healthcare IT industry. One that serves as an ever increasing backbone to our interconnected world. Many business spend hundreds of thousands of dollars on EMRs and various disparate healthcare systems. Many times these systems share the same data sets, from patient demographics and insurance information, to order sets and billing transactions. With over 10 years of HL7 interface development experience I can can help you ensure faster service and better patient outcomes.
